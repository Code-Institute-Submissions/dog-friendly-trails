Queen Maeve's Grave Loop 6km

Sligo

There are lots of No Dogs Allowed signs as you approach this climb from either the Strandhill side or the Glen Road side. But on climbing it you will encounter lots of dogs with their owners. Locals will tell you that these signs are always ignored and are meaningless. Do keep your dog on a lead though as there are sheep grazing at the top and on the slopes.

  • Leads Required: Yes at all times
  • Grade of Walk: Strenuous
  • Walking Terrain: Mountain Trail, Pathways
  • Clean Up POOP!

Visit: Sligo Walks Queen Maeve Trail - Knocknarea

Turraun Route Loop 15.8km

Canon Sheehan Loop 7km

Cork

After crossing the bridge you follow the loop along the left bank of the Ogeen River. You will cross two footbridges on this loop taking you from County Cork into County Limerick and then back to Cork again! Shortly afterwards the loop joins a forestry road. Keep an eye out towards the end of this stretch for a Mass Rock well preserved and still used on occasions today.

  • Leads Required: No
  • Grade of Walk: Easy
  • Walking Terrain: Forest tracks/paths and riverbank
  • Clean Up POOP!

Visit: Ballyhoura Walks Canon Sheehan Loop
